About Us

Sweda Petcare Inc is a company that researches and develops top-quality pet food products for cats and dogs. We love our furry friends, and the goal of our work is to bring love and care to our pet friends by making the best-quality and delicious food. Our pet food products are safe, nutritious, comprehensive and contains zero additives.

Our Products

We have researched and produced cat and dog food. Our food contains no additives and is 100% derived from wholesome natural ingredients, such as high-quality meat, seafood and plants. It meets the needs for growth in cats and dogs.

We use low-temperature baking technology to prevent the loss of nutrients in food processing and ultimately the food product maintains a good taste. Cats and dogs are crazy about our food product!

Our pet food is made 100% from raw natural ingredients and contains zero additives. Pet food without additives have several benefits, including reducing the risk of allergy and food intolerance, and boosting the digestive system health. Natural ingredients in our product are more easily absorbed and utilized by the digestive system of pets, reducing problems such as vomiting, diarrhea or constipation.

Why Zero-Additives?

  • Reduce allergies and food intolerance in pets.
  • More suitable for pets that are sensitive to additives.
  • Improve digestive system health.
  • Avoid potential harmful health risks.
  • Improve nutrients absorption.
  • Prevent behavioral problems.
  • Encourage sustainability.
  • Improve overall health and vitality of pets.
